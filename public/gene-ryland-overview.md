## 7. Legal & Compliance

### TSR Compliance (Telemarketing Sales Rule)
- Full compliance with FTC's Telemarketing Sales Rule (16 CFR Part 310)
- **No advance fees for credit repair services** — fees are only collected after work is completed
- All outbound calls restricted to 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM recipient's local time
- National Do Not Call Registry compliance
- Internal Do Not Call list maintained
- Full disclosures provided at the beginning of every call
